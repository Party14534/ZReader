THEORETICAL AND HISTORICAL PERSPECTIVES ON FAMILIES p. 567

Learning Objectives

Review the development of sociological thinking about families. Learn how the family has changed over the last 500 years, and the social and economic factors underlying these changes.

Terms to Know

primary socialization • personality stabilization • affective individualism
