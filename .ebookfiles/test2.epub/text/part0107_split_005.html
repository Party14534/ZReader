UNANSWERED QUESTIONS p. 641

Learning Objectives

Learn about some of the current educational issues that concern sociologists, including the debate over the sources of IQ, the merits of homeschooling, and the benefits of international education.

Concept Checks

  1. Explain the relationship between race and intelligence. Do you find the evidence compelling?
  2. What are three reasons parents decide to homeschool their children?
  3. Describe several characteristics of the “typical” international student enrolled at a U.S. college or university.
