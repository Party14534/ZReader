Glossary

primary socialization
The process by which children learn the cultural norms and expectations for behavior of the society into which they are born. Primary socialization occurs largely in the family.
personality stabilization
According to functionalist theory, the family plays a crucial role in assisting its adult members emotionally. Marriage is the arrangement through which personalities are supported and kept healthy.
affective individualism
The belief in romantic attachment as a basis for contracting marriage ties.
