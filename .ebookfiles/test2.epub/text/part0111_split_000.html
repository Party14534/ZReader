If classical theorists believed that religious beliefs were giving way to scientific thought and secularization, today it is clear that a religious revival has been occurring, both globally and in the United States. In our review of current research, we first present a current picture of trends in world religions and religion in the United States, before turning to two topics that are of particular interest to many sociologists of religion: gender and religion, and the global rise of religious nationalism.
