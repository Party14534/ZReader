Glossary

postindustrial society
A notion advocated by those who believe that processes of social change are taking us beyond the industrialized order. A postindustrial society is based on the production of information rather than on material goods. According to postindustrialists, we are currently experiencing a series of social changes as profound as those that initiated the industrial era some 200 years ago.
postmodern society
A technologically sophisticated society that is preoccupied with consumer goods and media images.
deglobalization
The reduction of global interdependence by making economies as local as possible.
