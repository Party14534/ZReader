THEORIES OF GLOBAL INEQUALITY p. 296

Learning Objectives

Learn several sociological theories for why some societies are wealthier than others, and understand the strengths and weaknesses of each.

Terms to Know

neoliberalism • modernization theory • dependency theories • core • periphery • colonialism • dependent development • global commodity chains • semiperiphery • world-systems theory • global capitalism • transnational capitalist class

Concept Checks

  1. Describe the main assumptions of neoliberal theories of global inequality.
  2. Why are dependency theories of global inequality often criticized?
  3. Compare and contrast core, peripheral, and semiperipheral nations.
  4. How do world-systems theory and the theory of global capitalism differ in their view of the role of the individual countries in the global economic system?
